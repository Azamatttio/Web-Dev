Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Ground beef 500g

Steps:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, brown the ground beef.
  3. Add chopped onions and garlic to the beef.
  4. Pour in tomato sauce and let it simmer.
